Considerations

Pros & Cons

Why Six Senses Douro Valley

  • Six Senses luxury brand - world-class service and amenities
  • UNESCO World Heritage Douro Valley setting
  • Exceptional spa and wellness for wedding party
  • All-inclusive F&B at €475/person covers everything
  • No full hotel buyout required for small weddings
  • ~60 rooms on-site for guest accommodation
  • Portugal is an affordable, stunning destination
  • World-class wine region for tastings and excursions
  • Beautiful outdoor ceremony and reception spaces

Things to Consider

  • High room minimum: 10 rooms at €1,500/night = €30,000 for 2 nights
  • Events must end by midnight without full buyout
  • Music limited to 80 dB without full buyout
  • Some areas may not be available without buyout
  • Wedding planner not included - quoted separately
  • 1.5-hour drive from Porto airport (no nearby airport)
  • Significantly higher cost than other venues considered
